Mole

Description: My Grandma's Secret Mole Recipe

My grandma Susy has a secret recipe that will blow almost anyone's socks off or in this case tastebuds.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Dona Maria Mole Sauce
  • Coca Cola Can
  • Abuelita Mexican Chocolate Tablets

Steps

  1. Heat the Dona Maria Mole Sauce in a skillet at medium high for 10 minutes
  2. Lower the skillet to low temperature and add the whole coca cola can
  3. After 10 minutes add the Abuelita chocolate tablets and let them melt
  4. Once chocolate is melted, mix the sauce with a wooden spoon for 5 minutes (still on low temperature)
  5. Preheat a separate skillet to medium temperature and put half a table spoon of olive oil
  6. After 5 minutes, add 2lbs of chicken on to the skillet and lower the temperature to low-medium heat
  7. Let the chicken cook for 25-30 minutes or once you see it is ready
  8. Once the chicken is ready, mix it in with the mole sauce and voila!
