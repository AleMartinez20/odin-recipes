Captain Crunch

Description: The Perfect Bowl of Captain Crunch

As a kid, I always prided myself on how good of a captain crunch cereal bowl I could pour. Here is my unmatched recipe

Ingredients

  • Milk (whichever type you prefer)
  • Captain Crunch Cereal Box(with berries of course)
  • Bananas (optional)

Steps

  1. Retrieve cold milk from the fridge....actually no, leave it in the fridge. Milk has to be as cold as possible
  2. Retrieve bowl from wherever you have your bowls....make it a big bowl, treat yourself
  3. Get box of cereal and pour 2 1/2 cups of captain crunch on to the bowl
  4. Get banana, peel banana, slice banana into slices, place sliced bananas on to bowl
  5. Finally! Pour cold milk on to bowl....ratio of milk to cereal? I prefer more cereal than milk but enough milk to where every cereal bit gets some milk lovin'
